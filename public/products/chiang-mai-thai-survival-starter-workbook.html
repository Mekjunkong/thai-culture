Teacher rule

Correct rhythm and confidence first. Do not overload beginners with grammar. The product promise is “usable today.”

Pronunciation rule

Romanization is a guide only. Use Thai script + rhythm chunks + human correction for better speaking.

Foundation

Survival Thai basics

Polite endings

ครับ khrap — commonly used by men.

ค่ะ kha — many statements by women.

คะ kha? — many questions by women.

Beginner formula

Request/action + thing/place + detail + polite ending

Example: ขอ + กาแฟเย็น + หวานน้อย + ครับ

FunctionThaiRomanUse when...
Helloสวัสดีครับ / ค่ะsà-wàt-dii khrap / khaEntering a shop, class, or condo office.
Thank youขอบคุณครับ / ค่ะkhàawp-khun khrap / khaEvery time someone helps.
Excuse me / sorryขอโทษครับ / ค่ะkhǎaw-thôot khrap / khaGetting attention politely.
Please speak slowlyพูดช้าๆ ได้ไหมครับ / คะphûut cháa-cháa dâai mái?When Thai comes back too fast.
A little Thaiพูดไทยนิดหน่อยphûut Thai nít-nòiLower pressure and create friendliness.

Mission 01

Cafe Thai

Outcome: Order one drink naturally

Base sentence

ขอกาแฟเย็นหวานน้อยครับ / ค่ะ

khǎaw gaa-fae yen wǎan nói khrap / kha

I would like iced coffee, less sweet, please.

Phrase bank

ThaiRomanMeaning
ขอกาแฟเย็นครับ / ค่ะkhǎaw gaa-fae yen khrap / khaI would like iced coffee.
หวานน้อยwǎan nóiLess sweet.
ไม่ใส่น้ำตาลmâi sài náam-dtaanNo sugar.
ใส่น้ำแข็งน้อยsài náam-khǎeng nóiLess ice.

Build your sentence

1 Request / action
2 Item / place
3 Detail
4 Polite ending

Write your personal sentence, then say it without looking.

Roleplay

Staffรับอะไรดีคะ?What would you like?
Studentขอกาแฟเย็นหวานน้อยครับ/ค่ะIced coffee, less sweet, please.
Staffหวานน้อยนะคะLess sweet, okay.
Studentครับ/ค่ะ ขอบคุณครับ/ค่ะYes, thank you.

Friendly Social Thai

ผม / ฉันชื่อ ___ อยู่เชียงใหม่ครับ / ค่ะ

My name is ___. I live/stay in Chiang Mai.

ThaiRomanMeaning
