# Design Direction

## Visual register
Product-led learning app with a warm cultural landing page. Design serves the course, but the homepage needs more personality than a default app shell.

## Palette
Use OKLCH colors and tinted neutrals.

- Jasmine cream: warm page surface.
- Deep indigo: trustworthy primary action and high-contrast text areas.
- Temple red: restrained emphasis for labels and cultural accents.
- Banana leaf green: success/progress and natural warmth.
- Turmeric gold: CTA highlights and small celebratory marks.
- Tamarind brown: warm dark neutral alternative to pure black.

Avoid raw black, raw white, pure gray, and generic blue gradients.

## Typography
Use the existing Inter stack for reliability and speed. Create distinction through scale, weight, tracking on labels, line length, and Thai/English phrase treatments rather than adding decorative fonts.

## Layout principles
- Homepage should feel like a guided path: promise, real situations, curriculum, paid-course offer.
- Use fewer generic cards; where cards remain, give them clear job-to-be-done and outcome copy.
- Use asymmetric hero composition with one memorable cultural learning artifact.
- Keep body copy to 65 to 75 characters where possible.
- Mobile first: large touch targets, stacked content, no hidden primary path.

## Components
- Rounded shapes are okay, but avoid nested-card sameness.
- Links and buttons need visible focus states and 44px touch targets.
- Week cards should include outcome, situation, status, and action affordance.
- Planned weeks should look planned, not broken links.

## Motion
Subtle state motion only: hover lift, focus rings, short ease-out transitions. Respect reduced motion.
