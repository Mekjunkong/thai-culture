# Thai Culture & Language

## Register
product

## Product purpose
Thai Culture & Language is a beginner Thai language course app for foreigners. It teaches practical Thai through cultural context so learners can speak politely, understand everyday social expectations, and feel comfortable in real situations in Thailand.

## Users
- Foreigners living in or visiting Thailand who want more than tourist phrasebook Thai.
- Absolute beginners who need confidence with greetings, wai, politeness particles, prices, market language, food ordering, and local etiquette.
- Learners who benefit from clear, structured lessons with cultural explanations, audio practice, and quizzes.

## Tone
Warm, practical, culturally respectful, reassuring, and human. The course should feel like a calm local friend explaining what to say and why it matters.

## Brand principles
- Culture makes the language make sense.
- Teach real-life confidence, not memorized phrases.
- Show Thai social context clearly without exoticizing it.
- Feel warmer than a generic SaaS course page, but stay trustworthy and easy to use.

## Anti-references
- Generic blue SaaS landing pages.
- Tourism brochure clichés.
- Loud neon Thailand nightlife aesthetics.
- Internal MVP language shown to learners.
- Decorative Thai motifs that do not support comprehension.

## Current product state
Week 1 and Week 2 are built and browsable. Week 1 covers greetings, wai, politeness particles, and mai pen rai. Week 2 covers numbers, prices, colors, and everyday objects. Supabase progress tracking and Stripe checkout are planned/ready in the app architecture.
