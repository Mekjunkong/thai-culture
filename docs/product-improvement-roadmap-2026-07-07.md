# Product Improvement Roadmap — Thai Lessons Chiang Mai

Date: 2026-07-07

## Product architecture

### Free layer
- Practice app
- 5-minute missions
- Downloadable starter workbook
- Goal: build trust and prove the method quickly

### Paid entry layer
- 30-minute speaking trial
- Goal: give one clear correction win and recommend the correct package

### Core product layer
- Starter Pack
- Includes: one lesson, workbook, practice missions, 7 days of voice-note correction
- Goal: turn a curious visitor into a real student

### Premium on-site layer
- Chiang Mai Mission Walk
- Cafe, market, restaurant, transport, condo, or social Thai in a real local context
- Goal: make the offline product feel special and local

### Retention layer
- 5-Lesson Survival Path
- Structured beginner path with homework and confidence tracker
- Goal: avoid random one-off lessons and create repeat revenue
